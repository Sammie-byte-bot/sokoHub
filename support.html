Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on SokoHub?

▼

Creating an account is simple and free. Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, enter your email address, create a secure password, and verify your email. You can then complete your profile by adding a profile picture, bio, and contact information. For sellers, additional verification steps may be required.

What payment methods does SokoHub accept?

▼

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), bank transfers, and digital wallets. All transactions are encrypted and processed securely through our payment partners.

How long does shipping take?

▼

Shipping times vary depending on the seller's location and the delivery method chosen. Standard delivery typically takes 3-7 business days within Kenya, while express delivery is 1-2 business days. International shipping times vary by destination. You can track your order in real-time through your account.

What is your refund policy?

▼

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for most items. If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, you can request a refund or replacement. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. Some items (like custom orders or clearance items) may have different policies.

How do I report a problem with my order?

▼

If you experience any issues with your order, go to "My Orders" in your account, select the order, and click "Report Issue." Describe the problem in detail and upload photos if applicable. Our support team will review your report within 24 hours and work with you to find a resolution.

Can I sell on SokoHub?

▼

Yes! Anyone can become a seller on SokoHub. You'll need to complete seller verification, which includes providing identification, business information, and bank details. Once approved, you can create listings, manage inventory, and start selling. We charge a small commission on each sale to cover platform costs.

Safety Tips for Buyers & Sellers

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to have a secure and positive experience on SokoHub.

🛡️ General Safety Guidelines

  • Never share personal information: Avoid sharing passwords, bank details, or sensitive information with other users.
  • Use secure passwords: Create strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication on your account.
  • Verify seller credentials: Check seller ratings, reviews, and verification badges before making purchases.
  • Use the SokoHub platform: Always communicate and transact through SokoHub's secure messaging and payment systems.
  • Report suspicious activity: If you notice anything unusual, report it immediately to our support team.

🛒 Buyer Safety

  • Check product details: Read descriptions carefully, view all photos, and check dimensions and specifications.
  • Review seller ratings: Look at previous buyer reviews and ratings to gauge seller reliability.
  • Use buyer protection: Our buyer protection program covers items not received or significantly not as described.
  • Document everything: Take screenshots of listings, messages, and transaction details for your records.
  • Inspect on delivery: Check items immediately upon delivery. Report any issues within 24 hours.
  • Meet safely: If arranging local meetups, choose public locations and bring a friend if possible.

📦 Seller Safety

  • Accurate descriptions: Provide detailed, honest product descriptions with clear photos from multiple angles.
  • Secure packaging: Package items securely to prevent damage during transit.
  • Track shipments: Always use tracked shipping methods and keep proof of delivery.
  • Verify buyers: Check buyer profiles and ratings before shipping high-value items.
  • Communicate clearly: Respond promptly to buyer inquiries and provide tracking information.
  • Keep records: Maintain records of all transactions, communications, and shipments for dispute resolution.

⚠️ Red Flags to Watch Out For

  • Requests to communicate outside the SokoHub platform
  • Unusually low prices for high-value items
  • Sellers with no reviews or verification badges
  • Requests for payment through untraceable methods
  • Pressure to complete transactions quickly
  • Requests to ship before payment is confirmed
  • Suspicious links or attachments in messages

🔐 Account Security

  • Change your password regularly (at least every 3 months)
  • Enable two-factor authentication for added security
  • Log out after each session, especially on shared devices
  • Don't use the same password on multiple platforms
  • Verify sender email addresses in communications
  • Be cautious of phishing emails claiming to be from SokoHub
⚠️ Important: SokoHub staff will never ask for your password or personal information via email or message. If you receive such requests, report them immediately.

Community Rules & Guidelines

SokoHub is a community marketplace built on trust, respect, and fair dealing. All members must adhere to these rules to maintain a safe and positive environment for everyone.

1. Prohibited Items & Content

The following items and content are strictly prohibited on SokoHub:

  • Counterfeit, stolen, or illegally obtained goods
  • Weapons, explosives, or dangerous materials
  • Drugs or controlled substances
  • Hazardous materials or chemicals
  • Hate speech, harassment, or discriminatory content
  • Sexually explicit or adult content
  • Intellectual property violations (pirated software, unauthorized copies)
  • Endangered species or animal cruelty products
  • Spam, scams, or fraudulent listings

2. Honest & Accurate Listings

  • Provide accurate product descriptions and specifications
  • Use clear, recent photos that accurately represent the item
  • Disclose any defects, damage, or wear
  • Don't use misleading titles or tags to attract buyers
  • Update listings if items are no longer available
  • Price items fairly and transparently

3. Professional Communication

  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Be polite, respectful, and professional in all communications
  • Don't harass, threaten, or abuse other users
  • Avoid spam or unsolicited promotional messages
  • Resolve disputes amicably and in good faith
  • Don't attempt to redirect transactions outside the platform

4. Fair Trading Practices

  • Honor agreed-upon prices and terms
  • Ship items as described and within the promised timeframe
  • Process refunds promptly when applicable
  • Don't engage in price gouging or unfair practices
  • Treat all buyers and sellers equally and fairly
  • Don't manipulate ratings or reviews

5. Review & Rating Integrity

  • Leave honest, accurate reviews based on your actual experience
  • Don't post fake reviews or ratings
  • Don't review items you haven't purchased or sellers you haven't transacted with
  • Don't ask buyers to leave positive reviews in exchange for discounts
  • Don't retaliate against negative reviews with threats or abuse

6. Account Responsibility

  • You are responsible for all activity on your account
  • Don't share your login credentials with others
  • Don't create multiple accounts to circumvent rules or manipulate the system
  • Provide accurate personal and business information
  • Notify us immediately if your account is compromised

7. Consequences of Violations

Violations of these community rules may result in:

  • Warning or suspension of your account
  • Removal of listings
  • Restriction from buying or selling
  • Permanent ban from the platform
  • Legal action if applicable
📢 Report Violations: If you witness a violation of these rules, please report it to our support team immediately. We take all reports seriously and investigate thoroughly.
